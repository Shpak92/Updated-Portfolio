Subman

Details

Having trouble managing your subscriptions? Unexpected automatic withdrawals putting your bank accounts in the negatives? Sounds like you need a subman! With Subman you are able to manage all of your subscriptions with ease. All you need to do is to login using your Google credientials, and then you are able to log in your subscriptions and updates when your bills are due!


Skills Used

  • Node.Js
  • Express
  • Passport.Js
  • Google OAuth2.0
  • Sequelize/MySQL
